The reccommended daily protein intake is 1.4-2g of protein per KG of bodyweight. This is recommended by multiple sources, Protein helps repair the muscle fibers and builds mass on the muscle. click here to find out more

The recommended amount of rest between each sets for strength and power growth is roughly around 2-5 Minutes, A nice rest between each set will allow you to work harder with each set, allowing you get more heavy repititions in and therefore putting your muscles under more pressure.
