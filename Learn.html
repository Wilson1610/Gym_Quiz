The recommended daily protein intake is 1.4-2g of protein per KG of bodyweight. This is recommended by multiple sources, Protein helps repair the muscle fibers and builds mass on the muscle. Protein is also the main source of nutrient to help muscles recover. click here to find out more

The recommended amount of rest between each sets for strength and power growth is roughly around 2-5 Minutes, A nice rest between each set will allow you to work harder with each set, allowing you get more heavy repititions in and therefore putting your muscles under more pressure. But rest between each set is all different depending on your goal, to learn more click here

There are multiple reasons as to why the eccentric motion of an exercise is as important as the concentric of a motion, it helps the put the muscle under more tension when controlling the repitition. It also helps you to perfect the form, as you are controlling the weight instead of just droppping it the muscle memory will kick in and overtime when using heavier weights your form will still be up to standard. The largest Muscle in the human body is the Gluteus Maximus, this is located at the back of the hip known as glutes/buttocks, which is mainly used when you climb stairs, hold body in standing position and getting into a standing postion. Read More

The recomended amount of calorie per day deficit for weight loss is roughly 500-700 calories a day, this will be different to each body type such as height and genetics. This can be reached by either cutting down your diet or through cardio or both Click here to read more
